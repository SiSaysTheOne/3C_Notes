# Notes

Two-Weapon Fighting: 
- When you attack with a light melee weapon, use a bonus action to attack with another light melee weapon. 
- Don’t add your ability modifier to the damage of the bonus attack.

When rolling to hit: Add proficiency and ability mod

When rolling damage: Add ability mod

Legendary protectors only procs on the first hit of a rage
