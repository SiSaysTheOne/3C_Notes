

The Donnovans
- The family we helped sort themselves into their coffins
- They were a family of skeletons who had recently died

The survivor
- Hungry, paranoid dude curled up in a crevice
- Gave them some of our rations, which they immediately devoured once we left

The Forsaken Pit
- Two goblins and two bugbears were in a pit
- We threw fire on the goblins, killing them
- Thanton drops a molly on one of the bug bears, willingly drops down and stabs the other with a crit through its skull
- The flaming one is immolated by allysum and falls into the campfire, burning to death
- The three are scarred for life

The respite room
- A room with small critters and beautiful vegetation
- Had to make charisma saving throws to resist the allure of this room and continue through the dungeon

The Fallen Kingdom
- The old king
	- We found the spirit of an old king, who was traumatized by the downfall of his nation
	- We successfully talk-no-jutsu persuaded him to allow us passage
- The Sphinx
	- Stone Sphinx which asked us a riddle
	- Each incorrect answer petrified one of us
	- We got down to our last party member before successfully solving the riddle
- Birdmaid
	- A lady sat on a bench, feeding birds
	- Asked us to give up something we valued for bird feed to feed the birds
	- Thant gave up a handaxe
	- Those who did not feed the bird were (later) swooped up by a large bird statue and dropped from a great height
