Proper backstory
- Father is a human homesteader, mother is an orc outlander
- Thant never actually talked to any of his orc-side family. His Mother tries not to think about them. When orcs do come by, he was almost always put out of earshot.
- Thant was 17 when he left home. He didn't really start putting pieces together until the Vampire mafia encounter. Their behaviors reminded him a lot of the orc band that would come by.
- The family did have a lot more money to spend whenever that particular band of orcs stopped coming by. They weren't really farm insurance, were they? They must have been debters of some sort.
- Being put into debt in the Pirate's Expanse fueled his disdain for this kind of person-- them debters-- them bastards. This is what inspired communing with the shadow weaver.
